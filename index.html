About

Lets go on a tour...

I have a BA in Economics from the University of Cape Coast. After my degree, I served as a teaching assistant in my department. I am the best alto singer in the world. When I grow up, I will be a techie. I am currently an Entrepreneur-in-Training at Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology. I love deeply and forgive easily.

Passion

"There is no passion to be found playing small - in settling for a life that is less than the one you are capable of living." Nelson Mandela.

  • Education

    Education facilitates enlightenment.

    Everyone needs education. It takes time to be educated but once you are in you just cant resist the need for growth. The crown of education is to be impactful in the society and be willing to make the world a better place. My dream is to see people of all race have quality education and be empowered to win.

  • Music

    The food for the soul

    Music heals! There is always that special rhythm with intriguing lyrics recorded specially for you. I am a part time singer but in my next life I will be a musician. My favourite artiste is Kurt Carr.

  • Children

    Little angels with bright future

    I loved my childhood days and I believe that it is that part of life that most lessons are learnt from. Whether you are a nativist or empericist, you would agree with me that, whatever shape a child is moulded into is what he or she becomes. Although we all eventually unlearn and relearn some of our attitudes and perceptions, that task is not a walk in the park. This is why children are dear to my heart. They are clean slates on which we can transfer knowledge, good habits, clear mentality and great values. They are fragile yet strong, innocent but foolish, silly but genuine.

  • Technology

    It's simply the best.

    I am a tech newbie. Tech keeps facinating. Tech products keep evolving and just when you think you've had enough, a better product pops up on the market. Tech makes life easier and I am amazed at the time I get to spend with my family with the help of tech. One last thing, tech makes life easier for techies tooo. Even for us programmers( like i said earlier, I am a newbie), tech provides easier and better ways for us to code. How cool is that? My dream is to build a global software company. Tech is definitely the path for me.
